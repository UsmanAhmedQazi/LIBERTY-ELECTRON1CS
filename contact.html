Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are your shipping options?
We offer standard delivery (3-5 business days) and express delivery (1-2 business days) across Pakistan. Free shipping is available on orders above Rs. 10,000.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 7-day return policy for most products. Items must be in original condition with all accessories and packaging. Please contact our support team to initiate a return.
Do you provide warranty on products?
Yes, all our products come with official manufacturer warranty. Warranty period varies by product - typically ranging from 6 months to 2 years.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash on delivery, credit/debit cards, online bank transfers, and mobile wallet payments. All online payments are processed through secure, encrypted channels.
